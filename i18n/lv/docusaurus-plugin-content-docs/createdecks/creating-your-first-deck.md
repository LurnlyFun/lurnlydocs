## 🪜 Step-by-Step Tutorial

### 1. Identify Your Deck
On the `Create-Deck.html` page, start with the **Settings Panel** on the left.
*   Enter a **Deck Name** (e.g., "World Capitals").
*   Pick a fun **Icon** to help it stand out on your dashboard.
*   *Need a deep dive? Check out [Deck Basics](./basics.md).*

### 2. Create Your First Card
Click the **➕ Add Card** button to open the editor.
*   **The Question:** Type your question in the text area.
*   **The Mode:** For your first time, leave it on **Normal** and **Flip**. 
*   **The Answer:** Type the answer in the **Correct Answer** box.
*   *Want to try Multiple Choice instead? Check out [Card Types](./card-types.md).*

### 3. Add Media for Memory
Visuals help you learn faster! 
*   Click the 🖼️ icon under **Add Media**.
*   Paste a link to an image.
*   Look at the **Live Preview** on the right to see how it looks!
*   *For video and audio tips, see [Adding Multimedia](./multimedia.md).*

### 4. Save and Review
*   Click **💾 Save** inside the card editor to add the card to your list.
*   Finally, click the large **💾 Save Deck** button at the bottom of the page.

## ✅ What's Next?
The `lurnly-create-deck.js` script will save your deck and whisk you back to your **Dashboard**. From there, just click your new deck to start studying!
