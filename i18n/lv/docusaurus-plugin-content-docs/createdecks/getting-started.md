# ✨ Getting Started

Ahh.. Welcome to Lurnly! Whether you are preparing for exams, learning a new language, or mastering a hobby, creating a custom flashcard deck is the most effective way to start your journey.

## 🧭 Navigating to the Creator

To begin building, you need to access the Deck Creation page. You can get there in two ways:

1. **Via the Dashboard:** Click on **Flashcards** in the top navigation menu to open your dashboard. Look under the **Quick Actions** section and click **Create Deck**.
2. **Direct Link:** Head straight to [lurnly.fun/dashboard](https://lurnly.fun/dashboard) and select the **Create Deck** action.
