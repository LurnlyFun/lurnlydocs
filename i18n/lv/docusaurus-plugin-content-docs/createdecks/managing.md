# 💾 Saving and Managing Cards

Once you start building your deck, you can easily manage, edit, and update your card list before finalizing the deck.

## 📋 The Card List
Every card you create appears in the **Cards List**. From here, you can:
* **Edit**: Click the ✏️ icon or the card itself to reload it into the editor.
* **Delete**: Click the 🗑️ icon to permanently remove a card from the deck.
* **Track Count**: You can add up to **100 cards** per deck.

## 💾 Saving Your Work
The logic handled by lurnly-create-deck.js ensures your work is saved correctly:
1. Ensure your deck has a name and at least one card.
2. Click the **Save Deck** button at the bottom of the page.
3. If you are editing an existing deck (via a URL edit parameter), the button will display **Update Deck** instead.

> **Success:** After saving, your deck is stored in your local library, and you will be redirected to your **Dashboard**.
