# 🏗️ Deck Basics

Setting up your first deck in Lurnly is simple. Before you start adding cards, you'll need to define the identity of your deck using the settings panel.

## ⚙️ Initial Settings
The left sidebar of the Create-Deck.html page allows you to configure the deck's core information:

* **Deck Name**: Give your deck a clear, descriptive title (e.g., "Spanish 101" or "Cell Biology").
* **Description**: (Optional) Provide a brief overview of what the deck covers to help you stay organized.
* **Author**: Your name will be saved in your browser for future decks by default.
* **Icon Selector**: Choose an emoji to represent your deck visually on your dashboard.

## ⏲️ Special Features
You can customize the learning experience using the **Special Features** section:

* **Enable Timer**: Set a 30 second timer for each card during your study session.
* **Shuffle Cards**: Toggle this on to present cards in a random order every time you study.

> **Note:** You must enter a **Deck Name** and add at least one card before the "Save Deck" button becomes active.
