# 🖼️ Enhancing with Media

You can make your cards more engaging by adding multimedia. Every card (Normal or Informational) supports one piece of media to provide visual or auditory context.

## Supported Media Types
In the **Add Media** section of the card editor, you can select one of the following:

* **🖼️ Image**: Paste a **URL** for an image to display it above the question or content.
* **🎥 Video**: Paste a **YouTube URL** to embed a video player directly into the card.
* **🎵 Audio**: Paste an audio file **URL** to include a playable sound clip.

## 👁️ Live Preview
As you add text or media, the **Live Preview** panel on the right side of the screen provides a real-time look at your card.
* It shows exactly how the card will appear to the learner.
* It updates instantly as you type or change media URLs.
* If you leave the fields blank, it will show a prompt to "Start typing to see preview."
